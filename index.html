FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Thekedaar work?
▾
Post your requirement with project details and location. Our system instantly matches you with verified local contractors. You receive multiple quotes, compare them, and hire the best fit — all within minutes.
How are contractors verified?
▾
Every contractor undergoes a 3-step verification: identity verification (Aadhaar/PAN), background check, and skills assessment. Only contractors who pass all three are listed on Thekedaar.
Is there any service charge for customers?
▾
Thekedaar is completely free for customers. Post requirements, get quotes, and hire — all at no cost. Contractors pay a small subscription or per-lead fee.
Can I compare multiple quotes?
▾
Yes! When you post a requirement, up to 5 contractors can submit quotes. You can compare them side-by-side based on price, timeline, rating, and experience before deciding.
How do payments work?
▾
Payments go through our secure escrow system. Funds are held safely and released to the contractor only after you confirm completion and satisfaction. Multiple payment methods supported.
Can contractors register for free?
▾
Yes, registration is completely free! Contractors can create their verified profile at no cost. There's a small fee only when you receive and accept leads from customers.
